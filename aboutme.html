About Me

One of my favorite passtimes as a child was figuring out the problems in the paperback logic books near the crosswords at the supermarket. I also love languages and cooking. To me programming is a perfect combination of these concepts: logic, syntax, planning, and creativity.

After obtaining a BA in Comparative Literature from Yale University, I worked in New York City as a producer at several big ad agencies. When I moved to Florida, I continued that role in an animation company. Project management and quiet leadership are second-nature to me.

I am now excited to use my logical and analytical sides to solve programming challenges for organizations that serve mental health or the environment.




In my spare time: Yoga - Gardening - Bass Guitar
